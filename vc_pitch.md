## 1. The Problem: The "Black Box" of Crypto
In traditional crypto exchanges (like FTX or Mt. Gox), you deposit your money into a "Black Box."
*   **You don't know** if they are insolvent.
*   **You don't know** if they are gambling with your funds.
*   **You can't withdraw** if they decide to freeze withdrawals.
You are forced to **trust** the humans running the exchange. Humans fail.

1.  **Deposit**: User sends ETH to the Vault Smart Contract.
2.  **Trade**: User clicks "Buy" on the dashboard.
